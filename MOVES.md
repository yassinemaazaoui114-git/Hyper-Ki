# Hyper Ki Legends — Move & Combo Guide

Everything a player can do, with inputs and sample combos.
Inputs use **Player 1 defaults** — rebind anything in Settings → Controls.
"toward" = the direction pointing at your opponent.

| | Player 1 | Player 2 |
|---|---|---|
| Move / dash | Arrows (double-tap = dash) | J L (double-tap) |
| Jump / Sidestep | ↑ / ↓ | I / K |
| Punch / Kick | A / S | U / O |
| Ki Blast (hold = Beam) | D | P |
| Charge Ki | Q | M |
| Guard | W | N |
| Transform / Ultimate | E / R | H / Y |

## Movement
| Move | Input | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Walk | ← / → | Turns to face automatically |
| Dash | double-tap ← or → | Fast approach; leaves afterimages |
| Jump | ↑ | Air attack available |
| Sidestep | ↓ | Dodges blasts & beams; brief i-frames |

## Basic attacks
| Move | Input | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Punch chain | A → A → A → A | 4 hits; the 4th launches (opens a Vanish window) |
| Kick chain | S → S | 2 hits; the 2nd launches (opens a Vanish window) |
| Launcher | toward + S | Pops the enemy up for a juggle |
| Pursuit | after launcher: A | Teleport up and spike them back down |
| Air attack | (in air) A or S | Combo continuation / anti-air |

## Ki & energy
| Move | Input | Cost | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Charge ki | hold Q | — | Fills the 7-segment gauge |
| Ki Blast | tap D | 0.25 | Cheap chip / poke |
| Signature Wave | toward + hold D | 1 bar | Chargeable; each character's unique projectile |
| Beam 1 | hold D | 3 bars | Screen-crossing energy wave |
| Beam 2 | hold ↓ + hold D | 4 bars | Stronger second beam |
| Deflect | strike an incoming blast with any attack | — | Sends it back faster (rallies possible) |

## Defense
| Move | Input | Cost | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Guard | hold W | — | Blocks; small chip damage |
| Sidestep | ↓ | — | Sidesteps energy attacks |
| Teleport Counter | tap W the instant a hit lands | 2 bars | Warp behind attacker for a free punish |
| Vanish | tap W right after a heavy launch sends you flying | 1+ bars | Warp behind them and counter-strike |

## Throws & heavies (beat turtling)
| Move | Input | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Throw / Guard Break | hold W + A | Unblockable; smashes through a blocker |
| Ki Cancel | tap W during your own attack | 0.5 bars; cuts recovery to extend combos |
| Charged Heavy | hold Q + hold A or S | Release for a guard-crushing blow (charge = more power) |

## Transformations & finishers
| Move | Input | Requirement | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Transform | E | 5 bars, not in Hyper | Advances your form (bigger stats, new aura) |
| Hyper Mode | A + S together | 3 bars | Red-aura power state |
| Dragon Rush | in Hyper: A + S again (close) | in Hyper | Cinematic guessing duel — win to keep rushing |
| Ultimate | in Hyper: R | Hyper + 5 bars | 3-round arrow duel → cinematic finisher |
| Beam Clash | two beams collide | — | Mash A/Punch to overpower — loser eats big damage |

## States that happen TO you
| State | Cause | Escape |
|---|---|---|
| Hurt / Launched | getting hit | wait it out, or Vanish out of heavy launches |
| Dizzy | a long combo (~150 dmg) then a grounded hit | mash any button to recover; ~10s immunity after |

## Sample combos

**Bread & butter (0 meter)**
`A A A A` — the basic 4-punch string into a launch.

**Launcher juggle (0 meter)**
`toward+S` (launch) → `A` (pursuit spike). Simple, reliable, no meter.

**Kick confirm**
`S S` → the 2nd kick launches → `A` pursuit.

**Ki-cancel extension (needs 0.5+ bar)**
`A A A` → `W` (ki cancel) → `A A A A` — the cancel resets your string so you
keep the pressure. Chain more cancels while you have ki.

**Cancel into throw (mix-up)**
`A A` → `W` (cancel) → they block expecting more → `W+A` (throw/guard break).

**Charged-heavy punish (vs a turtle)**
`hold Q + hold S`, release → **GUARD CRUSH** stagger → `A A A A` free combo.

**Dizzy punish (after a long combo)**
Land a big combo → opponent goes **DIZZY** → `hold Q + hold A` charged heavy,
or go straight for `R` Ultimate if you're in Hyper.

**Meter dump (in Hyper)**
`A+S` (Hyper) → pressure into `A+S` again for **Dragon Rush**, OR build to full
and `R` for the **Ultimate** arrow duel.

**Vanish battle (defensive read)**
When a heavy hit launches you, `W` to **Vanish** behind them and counter — they
can Vanish yours back; each trade costs more ki until someone runs dry.

## Tips
- **Ki is one shared resource** for offense (beams, ultimate) and defense
  (teleport counter, vanish, cancels). Spending it all on offense leaves you
  defenceless — and vice-versa.
- **Turtling loses** to Throw (W+A) and Charged Heavy (Q+attack). If someone
  blocks forever, crack them.
- **Sidestep** (↓) beats projectiles but not melee — read your opponent.
- **Practice all of this in TRAINING MODE** (title menu): infinite time, a dummy
  that heals back to full, so you can drill any route.
